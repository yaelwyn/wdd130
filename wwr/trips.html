Detailed Trip Experiences

The Grand Canyon Multi-Day Expedition

Experience the ultimate adventure! This 7-day, 6-night journey takes you deep into the canyon's heart, offering world-class rapids, stunning geology, and tranquil nights under the stars. All meals and gear are included. Must be 18+ or 16+ with a guardian.

  • **Duration:** 7 Days / 6 Nights
  • **Difficulty:** Advanced (Class IV/V Rapids)
  • **Best Season:** Spring & Fall

The Devil's Tooth Thrill Run

A fast-paced, high-adrenaline single-day trip perfect for experienced rafters. Navigate continuous Class III and IV rapids in the scenic gorge. Includes lunch and all safety gear. Not recommended for children under 14.

  • **Duration:** Full Day (6 hours on water)
  • **Difficulty:** Intermediate/Advanced (Class III/IV)
  • **Best Season:** Early Summer

The Serene Family Float

A gentle, half-day introduction to rafting on Class I and II waters. Ideal for families with young children or those looking for a relaxing scenic tour. Enjoy swimming and wildlife viewing. Suitable for all ages (3+).

  • **Duration:** Half Day (3 hours on water)
  • **Difficulty:** Beginner (Class I/II)
  • **Best Season:** Mid-Summer
